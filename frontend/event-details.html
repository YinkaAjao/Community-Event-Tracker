Workshop Upcoming

Community Gardening Workshop

July 15, 2023
10:00 AM - 12:00 PM
Downtown Community Center

About This Event

Join us for a hands-on gardening workshop where you'll learn sustainable practices from local experts. This workshop is perfect for beginners and experienced gardeners alike who want to enhance their skills and learn about environmentally friendly gardening techniques.

Our experienced instructors will guide you through:

  • Soil preparation and maintenance
  • Planting techniques for various vegetables and herbs
  • Water conservation methods
  • Natural pest control solutions
  • Composting basics

All materials and tools will be provided. Just bring your enthusiasm and be prepared to get your hands dirty! Each participant will take home a starter plant to begin their own garden.

Who Should Attend

This workshop is designed for community members of all ages and experience levels. Whether you have a large backyard garden or just a few pots on your balcony, you'll learn valuable tips to enhance your growing space.

Organizer

Green Thumb Society

A local organization dedicated to promoting sustainable gardening practices and community engagement through nature.
